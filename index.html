Tote Bags

My commitment to sharing my art in unique ways recently led me to a rewarding project: painting tote bags. I wanted my creations to be seen and used in everyday life, so I chose the tote bag as the perfect functional canvas. This project has been an exciting way to transform a simple accessory into a mobile piece of artwork, effectively merging my passion for painting with the desire to bring my artistic style into practical, everyday expression. I take commissions for custom pieces too!
